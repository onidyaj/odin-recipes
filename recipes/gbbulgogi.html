Ground Beef Bulgogi Recipe

Ingredients

  • Your choice of ground beef
  • Soy Sauce
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Honey or Brown Sugar
  • Chili Oil
  • Sesame Oil
  1. Begin by frying your ground beef on a medium saucepan. Optionally you may saute onions to accompany the beef for flavor and texture. Occasionally stir the beef for even browning until 70% cooked.
  2. Add all of your seasonings to the beef and stir to evenly distribute.
  3. Finish with any garnishings or serve on a bed of rice.
