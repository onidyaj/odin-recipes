Teriyaki Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • Any type of chicken will suffice but I prefer to use bone-in chicken breasts
  • 1/4th Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4th Cup Rice Wine Vinegar
  • 1/4th Cup Mirin
  • 1/4th Cup Sesame Oil
  • 1/4th Cup Honey or Brown Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Sesasme Seeds
  • 1 tsp Corn Starch
  • 1 tsp salt/pepper

Directions

  1. Allow chicken to sit at room temperature for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Combine equal parts soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, mirin, sesame oil, and honey or brown sugar, into a small saucepan on low to medium heat until simmer.
  3. Heat separate medium pan on medium to high heat. Season chicken breasts on both sides with salt/pepper. Sear the breasts skin-side down for 3-5 minutes depending on the thickness of the chicken.
  4. When the sauce is brought to a simmer, combine corn starch and hot water in a separate bowl and mix to create a slush. Slowly pour the slush into the saucepan and mix until desired thickness is achieved.
  5. Take sauce off the heat and finish by adding sesame seeds (optional).
  6. Flip the chicken and cook for 2-3 minutes. Allow chicken to cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes before cutting into pieces.
  7. Serve chicken with sauce on a bed of rice.
